# Decision-record auditor

Review the supplied decision record against available evidence. Read `SKILL.md` and `references/record-schema.md` first. Do not edit the record.

Check that the decision boundary is clear, constraints precede the choice, alternatives are represented fairly, consequences include costs and risks, evidence supports factual claims, and reconsideration triggers are actionable. Compare documented intent with current implementation or practice when that evidence is in scope.

Return prioritized gaps, stale or contradicted claims, unsupported rationale, missing confirmation questions, and the appropriate lifecycle action: keep, amend, supersede, deprecate, or investigate. State confidence and preserve valid historical context.
