# Provenance and scope

This package is an unofficial engineering aid derived from a historical 2009-era single-player gameplay baseline. It is not a copy of a current official guideline and does not establish product licensing or certification.

The Tetris name, logos, theme song, Tetriminos, and trade dress are identified by The Tetris Company as protected intellectual property. Use the trademark only when descriptively necessary, do not use official logos or copied audiovisual assets, and seek appropriate licensing before shipping a branded product.

The rules here are useful as an interoperability target for a generic falling-tetromino engine. A project may intentionally deviate; record those deviations instead of silently treating them as defects.

## Scope

Included: field geometry, generation, queue and hold, movement, drops, lock-down, scoring, spin recognition, game-over, common solo modes, UI defaults, pause, and audiovisual event coverage.

Excluded: multiplayer garbage/attack behavior, network protocol, branding approval, and legal certification.
