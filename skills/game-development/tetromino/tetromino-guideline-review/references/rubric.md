# Weighted rubric

| Category | Rule weights | Maximum |
|---|---|---:|
| Core engine | ENG-01 6, ENG-02 4, ENG-03 6, ENG-04 5, ENG-05 5, ENG-06 4 | 30 |
| Scoring and spins | SCR-01 6, SCR-02 4, SCR-03 6, SCR-04 5, SCR-05 4 | 25 |
| Controls and lock-down | CTL-01 4, CTL-02 3, CTL-03 4, CTL-04 5, CTL-05 4 | 20 |
| Game over and modes | GOV-01 4, GOV-02 3, GOV-03 1, GOV-04 2 | 10 |
| UI and effects | UIE-01 4, UIE-02 4, UIE-03 3, UIE-04 2, UIE-05 2 | 15 |

PASS receives full weight; FAIL receives zero. UNKNOWN receives 40% under balanced scoring and 20% under strict scoring.

Verdicts: more than half UNKNOWN means insufficient evidence; otherwise 90–100 is high alignment, 75–89 moderate alignment, and below 75 low alignment. Scores describe alignment with this baseline, not overall game quality or official certification.
