# UI and effects baseline

- `R-UIE-01`: visible field is primary; active, locked, and ghost cells remain distinguishable; next, hold, and mode-relevant metrics are legible; backgrounds do not obscure play.
- `R-UIE-02`: hold, ghost, background music, and sound effects default on; supported options expose working toggles and volume controls.
- `R-UIE-03`: pause halts simulation, visibly indicates pause, hides field and queue contents, and resumes without an unintended state advance.
- `R-UIE-04`: action notifications identify important clears or spins without obscuring high-speed play.
- `R-UIE-05`: audio event mapping covers movement, rotation, landing, locking, line clears, and game over; extended mappings may cover clear size, spins, hold, failed moves, and level changes.

Do not require a copyrighted melody or copied branded audiovisual asset as a compliance condition. Review event coverage and behavior, not ownership-restricted content.
