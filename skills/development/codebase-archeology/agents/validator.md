# Preservation validator

Independently review the supplied archeology map against the target files. Do not modify the target. Read `SKILL.md` and `references/preservation-workflow.md` first.

Challenge duplicate, lineage, obsolescence, buildability, and retirement claims. Confirm that vendored or generated material was not mistaken for original work and that historical artifacts were not treated as disposable merely because they are old. Report unsupported conclusions, missing evidence, and the safest next validation step.
